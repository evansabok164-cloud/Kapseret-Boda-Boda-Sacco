About

Evans Abok
Founder & Developer

Pioneer Kapseret Boda Boda Sacco is a savings and credit cooperative built for boda boda riders in Kapseret, Eldoret. It exists to help members save consistently, access fair loans, support one another through the Welfare Fund, and run their sacco transparently — from savings and loan tracking to meetings, elections, and dividends.

This app was built to bring that office register onto every member's phone: a digital passbook that's always up to date, backed up, and accessible to the people who run and rely on the sacco.
